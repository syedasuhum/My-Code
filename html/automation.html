What is Automation?

Automation describes a wide range of technologies that reduce human intervention in processes, namely by predetermining decision criteria, subprocess relationships, and related actions, as well as embodying those predeterminations in machines.

Research and Innovation

Automated Pollination Robots Support Bees

The B-Droid is a robotic bee that can fly between flowers to pollinate plants. The robot evolved from a ground-based unit to a tiny quadcopter that can operate autonomously over a predetermined area. The B-Droids uses onboard cameras and an external computer to plan a flight path as they pollinate the crop. The technology was developed from programming, aerodynamics, robotics, automation and electronics

Automated System Translates between Sign Language and English

The computer that has been synced to the system then uses a natural language processing(NLP) module to translate the signer's movements into English, is the process of creating the world’s first fully automated sign language translation system. The sign language is translated into fully formed, this only support for grammatically correct sentences for a hearing person to read
