Customer Service Automation

Customer service automation is a customer support process that reduces human involvement in solving customer inquiries. Businesses achieve automated customer service using self-service resources, proactive messaging, or simulated chat conversations.

Consumer Service Automation Strategies

Add Live Chat to Allow Immediate Engagement

We can add live chat to a website and monitor the conversations. Get a feel for the topics and ways in which your team responds. Live chat is to offer an automated chatbot to handle inquiries outside of business hours. After you perfect the right responses, chatbots can function as standalone virtual agents on your website.

Update Announcements on your Phone System

It’s most often the first path customers take to get immediate help. It’s easy to provide notifications for incoming support calls. When customers call, they’ll appreciate that you’re actively working on their problem. A proactive notification on your phone system can do wonders for your customer experience.

Encourage Self-Service with a Useful Knowledge Base

Audit your support content regularly for accuracy, readability, and findability. Performing frequent quality assurance audits will flag articles in need of revisions. A web accessibility service can monitor your site for areas to improve. Videos can have support team review the content often for accuracy.

Make Use of Templated Responses

Offering editable responses can be advantageous to your team to save time and increase individual care to customers. Customer service managers can craft informative answers to the most frequently asked questions. Support agents can use those templates to reply the customers and coustomers services.

Integrate Customer Service Automation into your CRM

Most customer service tools operate independently from other business applications. On top of that, they primarily respond to inbound customer service inquiries. CRM software now offers integrations that can trigger automated sequences along the customer journey.

Survey Customers After Every Interaction

You can’t improve what you don’t measure, that why you should incorporate real-time customer feedback to your customer service strategy. can trigger an escalation based on how a customer responds. If they left a one-star rating and angry comments, schedule a call to customer.

Automation Trends in Electronics

Internet of Things(IOT)

It’s the idea that more than just a computer or smart phone can be connected to the internet. Electronics manufacturers are at the heart of making it all work, seamlessly integrating sensors, connectivity, and the web. Sensors are abundant in today’s electronics factories, from tool center points to motors and beyond. Examples voice assistants, cars etc.

Artifical Intelligence(AI)

The data generated by all the inventory moving across a floor and the robotics performing thousands of repetitive tasks make for a manufacturing environment that’s rich in AI potential. Most electronics manufacturers fit that bill. Any abnormalities, improvements or trends in performance can not only be identified, but specifically diagnosed.

Additive Manufacturing(3D)

Additive Manufacturing also known as 3D printing, additive manufacturing has made the once time-consuming and expensive prototyping phase much more efficient. Instead of machining tiny molds or delicate parts, these detailed pieces can be built into near-finished components. It enabled innovative chip designs and chips that can’t be recreated
