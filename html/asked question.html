Proxy Access

What is proxy Access?
Proxy access allow you to share your health record or gain access to other persons health record through your AppleWellness health account. Proxy access is helpful if you manage the case of a child or another adult. When you request proxy access to a child or other interact health record you will be able to access many of the same online features you do with your own account.
There are different type of proxy access available:
  • Proxy for children(0-12 years) is designed to help you manage your children's health Care needs. As proxy you will have to access to the information in the children's health record.
  • Proxy access for teens(13-17 years) provide Limited access to the teens health record. As proxy you will not have access to the information about the confidential medical service including pregnancy or reproductive counselling drugs and alcohol treatment etc
  • Proxy access for adults(18 and older) enables you to access and adults health record such as spouse or parent. As proxy you will have full access to the information in the adults record.
How do I get access to another adult's interactive health record?
To request access to another adult health record complete the adult proxy form and submit using the address or fax number has shown on the form.

What are visit summary and notes?
When you come to AppleWellness for a clinic appointment you are provided make a record of your visit in your health record this is viewable in your AppleWellness health account as a visit summary and notes.

Visit summary includes your care plan medication changes and any follow up instructions.
Notes include information that you have share during the visit as well as your provider thoughts about your diagnosis and treatment.

Sharing this information help you make sure that you and your Healthcare themes are always on the same page. It also allows you to review that was discuss and your treatment plan including changes in your medication, testing, appointment or any referrals needed.
AppleWellness health account patient can see visit summary and notes from the clinic visit with primary care, specially care and mental health providers.
